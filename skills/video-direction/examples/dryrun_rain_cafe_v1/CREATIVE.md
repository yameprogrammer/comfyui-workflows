## 1. One-image pitch

Rain stitches the café window into vertical silver lines. A young woman in a short summer skirt sits half-turned to an empty second seat, iced americano sweating between both hands, smile bright enough to sell “I’m fine” while the glass behind her keeps crying.

## 2. Central paradox

She performs sunshine for a problem that only the rain is allowed to show.

## 3. Genre of feeling

Hybrid café confession short: hospitality warmth on the face, weather grief in the frame — not melodrama breakdown, not pure comedy.

## 4. Visual world

- **palette:** warm wood + amber practicals · cool cyan window · ceramic white cup · one soft yellow (straw sleeve / distant umbrella tip)  
- **light:** soft warm key from interior lamp (cam-left), cool soft fill from window, light rim from street neon wet  
- **texture:** condensation, rain streaks, wood grain, cotton blouse  
- **banned:** pure black crushed faces, random multicolor neon rainbow, readable gibberish Hangul menus, sunny exterior cheat

## 6. Performance grammar

- **Lips:** S02, S04, S07 — si2v; micro nod only; no big lean  
- **Eyes:** down to cup (shame) → up to empty chair / guest (performance of ok)  
- **Hands:** both hands on cup as SI2V anchor; insert for straw turn  
- **Body:** open to table, slight closed shoulder to window cold  

## 7. Hook architecture

- **0–1.5s (S01):** tall rain window + tiny figure + empty chair — scroll stop without face spam  
- **Arc:** establish geography → soft confession lines → prop business → smile crack → release on residual rain  
- **Kill shot (end feeling):** viewer feels the weather told the truth her smile wouldn’t  

## 8. Thumbnail thesis

Vertical rain glass + warm cup hands mid-frame; face optional small — “pretty café, wet honesty.”

## 9. Anti-list

1. Face CU only wall for all dialogue  
2. Lyric/dialogue illustrated as on-screen text dump  
3. Same MS eye-level three times  
4. Freeze-pad last 2s of any clip  
5. Giant/tiny cup scale  
6. Readable fake menu Korean soup  
7. Umbrella motif spam every cut (umbrella only residual optional — not forced)  
8. Stand-up mid si2v line
