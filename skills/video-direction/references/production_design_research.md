## 2. Synthesis principles

1. **World one-liner first** — density and props follow emotion.  
2. **Motifs are staged**, not mentioned once in a pitch.  
3. **Sparse named anchors** beat clutter lists for AI.  
4. **Continuity is design**, not afterthought.  
5. **Graphics policy** prevents gibberish logos.  
6. **Factory packs** are SSOT for char/loc when present.
