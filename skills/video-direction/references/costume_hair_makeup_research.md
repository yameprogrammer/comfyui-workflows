## 2. Synthesis principles

1. **CHM is direction + continuity**, not only fashion moodboards.  
2. **Factory owns identity assets**; skill owns episode states.  
3. **Light interacts with MU** (shimmer, mask-like base).  
4. **Weather owns hair state** as much as style.  
5. **Shot size filters** what CHM must deliver.  
6. **AI:** describe once on stills; freeze look across motion.
