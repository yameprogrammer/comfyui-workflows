## 2. What changed over time (evolution → skill stance)

| Era / shift | Camera language change | Our stance |
|-------------|------------------------|------------|
| Classical continuity | Invisible camera, coverage | Still default for talking/story (R01/R02) |
| New Hollywood / “Master Shots” culture | Bold motivated angles on any budget | Intent-first angle/lens |
| Music video MTV→now | Kinetic moves, size events | Chorus event; still one-move per AI clip |
| Phone vertical era | 9:16 grammar, UI chrome, scroll hook | §8 mandatory for shorts |
| AI gen 2023–2026 | Stability > complexity | One move, simpler optics, QA |

Skill must teach **classic grammar + vertical + AI limits** together.

---

## 3. Synthesis principles baked into the guide

1. **Motivation before vocabulary** — labels without intent are noise.  
2. **Coverage before hero porn** — A–D package for editability.  
3. **One move / one idea** — AI + clear storytelling.  
4. **Lens is narrative distance** — 35 story, 85 isolate.  
5. **Vertical is a composition system** — not cropped cinema.  
6. **Rhythm rules stay factory R1–R6** — camera choices implement them.  
7. **Risk-aware sizes** — feet/hands/glass tagged.
