## 2. Principles

1. **Time is designed** before generation.  
2. **Spatial rhythm (size) + temporal rhythm (seconds)** both required.  
3. **Peaks and valleys** beat flat energy.  
4. **Audio proposes cuts; picture must agree.**  
5. AI forces **honest lengths** (split, don’t pad).
