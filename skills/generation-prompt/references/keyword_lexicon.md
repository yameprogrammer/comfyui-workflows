# Keyword lexicon (image & video)

Use as **building blocks**, not comma spam. Pick 1 per category unless stacking materials.

---

## Shot size

extreme long shot, long shot, full body, medium long shot, medium shot, medium close-up, close-up, extreme close-up, insert, over-the-shoulder, POV, two-shot

## Angle

eye level, low angle, high angle, top-down, dutch angle (rare), worm's-eye (rare)

## Lens feel

24mm wide environmental, 35mm natural storytelling, 50mm neutral, 85mm portrait compression, shallow depth of field, deep focus, soft bokeh, eyes sharp

## Camera move (motion prompts — pick one)

static camera, locked tripod, slow push-in, gentle dolly in, slow pull-out, pan left, pan right, tilt up, tilt down, tracking shot, handheld micro, slow orbit (rare)

## Light quality

soft key, hard key, diffused overcast, golden hour warm low sun, cool moonlight, practical lamp light, neon rim light, negative fill, high-key bright, low-key moody, Rembrandt soft cheek shadow

## Light direction

key from camera left, key from camera right, backlight rim, top light, underlight (horror rare), window light as fill

## Composition

rule of thirds, subject on left third, subject on right third, centered composition, large negative space, lead room, looking room, tight headroom, frame within frame, foreground bokeh, layered depth

## Materials / texture

natural skin texture not plastic, cotton weave, knit fabric, silk sheen, wet asphalt reflections, rain-streaked glass, condensation on ceramic, wood grain, brushed metal, matte plastic, soft film grain

## Optical feel

clean digital, light film grain, medium film grain, soft bloom (rare), hazy atmosphere (rare), anamorphic streak feel (MV rare)

## Motion body (I2V)

subtle breathing, slow blink, slight head turn, walk cycle start, fabric drift, steam rise, rain continuous, sip from cup, turn object in hands

## Speech (SI2V)

natural speech mouth motion, lip sync to dialogue, micro nod, soft smile while speaking, jaw motion, shoulders almost still

## Negatives (bank)

warp, identity morph, freeze frame, flicker, extra fingers, extra limbs, deformed hands, plastic skin, face melt, gibberish text, watermark, logo spam, whip pan, morphing background
